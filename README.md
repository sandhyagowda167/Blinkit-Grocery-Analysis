**📊 Blinkit Grocery Data Analysis
🏢 Project Overview**
This project focuses on analyzing grocery sales data from Blinkit (India’s quick-commerce platform) to gain insights into sales performance, customer preferences, and outlet efficiency. The goal is to help improve business decision-making through data-driven insights.
**KPI's Requirements**	
Total Sales	The overall revenue generated from all items sold.
Avreage Sales	The average revenue per sale.
Number of Items	The total count of different items sold.
Avreage Rating	The average customers rating for items sold.
**Business Requirements**
To conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution to identify key insights and opprtunities for optimization using various KPIs visualization in Power BI.
